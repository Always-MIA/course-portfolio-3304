Top 5 Travel Destinations

    2. Tokyo has a vibrant culture and amazing food. There was a lot to do and the people there know how to party!

  5. San Jose, Costa Rica

    6. San Jose, the capital city of Costa Rica, has a vibrant culture, beautiful parks, and proximity to stunning natural attractions.
